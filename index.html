Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a scan take?

Most residential properties take 1-2 hours. Larger commercial spaces may take 3-4 hours.

When will my tour be ready?

Standard turnaround is 24-48 hours. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

How do I share the tour?

You'll receive a unique URL that works on any device. Embed it on your website or share on social media.

Do I need to prepare the property?

We recommend clean, staged spaces with all lights on and blinds open.

How long is the tour hosted?

Standard hosting is included for 12 months with renewal options available, or transfer to your own Matterport account for free.

Can you add aerial/drone footage?

Yes! We're FAA Part 107 certified for commercial drone operations.
