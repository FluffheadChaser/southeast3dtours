Professional virtual tour services for historic homes, waterfront properties, and vacation rentals throughout the Charleston Lowcountry.

Capturing the Charleston Charm

Charleston's real estate market is one of the most desirable in the Southeast. From historic single houses downtown to beachfront properties on Sullivan's Island, waterfront homes in Mount Pleasant, and vacation rentals across the Lowcountry—your properties deserve marketing that captures their unique character and location.

We provide professional Matterport 3D tours throughout Charleston County and the surrounding Lowcountry, helping you showcase properties to local buyers and the many people relocating to the Charleston area from across the country.

Why 3D Tours Matter in Charleston

Historic Properties

Charleston's historic homes have unique architectural details, layouts, and charm that photos can't fully convey. 3D tours let buyers experience the flow and character of these special properties.

Vacation Rental Market

Charleston is a top tourist destination. Virtual tours build trust with guests, reduce questions, and help your property stand out on vacation rental platforms.

Remote Buyers

Many Charleston buyers are relocating from out of state. Give them the ability to tour properties remotely before scheduling in-person visits, saving time for everyone.

Waterfront & Beach Properties

Coastal properties command premium prices and deserve premium marketing. Showcase waterfront views, outdoor spaces, and beach access with immersive virtual tours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a travel fee for Charleston?

Yes. Charleston is outside our standard service area, so a travel fee applies. Contact us for exact pricing — it's based on location and travel time, and we're upfront about the cost before you commit.

Is the travel fee worth it for Charleston listings?

For most Charleston properties, yes. Historic homes, waterfront properties, and vacation rentals in the Lowcountry command premium prices where professional marketing investment pays off quickly. Contact us to discuss whether it makes sense for your specific listing.

Do you scan historic homes in the Charleston Historic District?

Yes. We've scanned multi-story historic properties, single houses, and commercial buildings. Matterport handles older and irregular architecture well and is an increasingly popular tool among agents marketing downtown Charleston properties.

Do you cover vacation rental properties on the barrier islands?

Yes. Folly Beach, Isle of Palms, Sullivan's Island, and other island properties are within reach — travel fee and logistics vary by location, so contact us to plan it out and confirm availability.
