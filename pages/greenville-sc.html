Matterport 3D Tours in Greenville & the Upstate SC

Professional virtual tour services for real estate, commercial properties, and vacation rentals throughout Greenville County, Spartanburg County, and the greater Upstate.

Your Local Matterport Partner Across the Upstate

Based in Travelers Rest, we're right in the middle of Upstate South Carolina — and it shows in our response times. We understand the unique character of properties across the entire region: historic homes in downtown Greenville and Spartanburg's Converse Heights neighborhood, luxury estates on Paris Mountain, new construction in Simpsonville and Mauldin, commercial spaces along the I-85 corridor, and vacation rentals near the mountains.

As a locally-based Matterport service provider, we offer fast response times and same-day or next-day availability throughout Greenville County, Spartanburg County, and the surrounding Upstate region. No travel fee for the vast majority of Upstate properties — just professional work delivered in 24–48 hours.

Why 3D Tours Matter in the Upstate

Fast-Moving Regional Market

Upstate South Carolina is attracting out-of-state buyers at a consistent pace. From Greenville's booming downtown to Spartanburg's growing neighborhoods, buyers are often searching remotely before they visit in person. A 3D tour lets them explore properties in detail and arrive ready to make a decision.

New Construction & Builder Marketing

Simpsonville and Mauldin are among the most active new construction markets in the entire Upstate. Builders use Matterport to showcase model homes and completed spec properties to buyers who want to preview floor plans and finishes before scheduling a site visit.

Commercial & Industrial Expansion

As companies continue to expand along the I-85 corridor — anchored by BMW manufacturing in Spartanburg and a growing West End district in Greenville — demand for commercial real estate is rising. Virtual tours help lease office, retail, and industrial spaces faster by giving prospects a thorough walkthrough before committing.

Historic Neighborhoods & Unique Properties

From the craftsman bungalows of Spartanburg's Converse Heights to Greenville's North Main and the luxury estates of Paris Mountain, the Upstate is full of properties with character worth showcasing. Matterport captures architectural details and spatial flow that standard photos simply can't convey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a travel fee for Upstate properties?

No — we're based in Travelers Rest, right in the heart of the Upstate. There's no travel fee for properties throughout Greenville County, Spartanburg County, or most of the surrounding region.

How quickly can I get a scan scheduled?

We typically offer same-day or next-day scheduling throughout the Upstate. Most scans are completed and delivered within 24–48 hours of the appointment. If you need a faster turnaround, call us directly.

Do you work with builders on new construction in Simpsonville or Mauldin?

Yes. Builders and developers use Matterport to showcase model homes and completed spec properties. It's an effective way to market to out-of-area buyers who want to preview the floor plan and finishes before scheduling a site visit.

Can you scan commercial and industrial properties along the I-85 corridor?

Yes. We regularly scan office buildings, retail spaces, industrial properties, and warehouses throughout the Spartanburg and Greenville corridors. Large or complex spaces may require a custom quote — contact us to discuss your specific needs.

Do Upstate MLS systems accept Matterport virtual tours?

Yes. Matterport generates a shareable link and embed code that works with all major MLS platforms used in the Greenville, Spartanburg, and Upstate markets.

What kinds of properties do you scan in the Upstate?

Everything from downtown condos and North Main bungalows to luxury estates in Travelers Rest, historic homes in Converse Heights, new construction in Simpsonville, commercial spaces in the West End, and industrial properties along I-85. If it has interior spaces worth showing, we can scan it.
