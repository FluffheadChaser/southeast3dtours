Questions Vacation Rental Owners Ask Us

Can I add the 3D tour to my VRBO or direct booking listing?

Yes. VRBO supports direct embedding so your tour appears right inside the listing. For other platforms, you receive a direct URL you can paste into your listing description or share anywhere — Booking.com, your direct booking website, social media, and more. Guests click the link and immediately start walking through your property. No app downloads required.

How should I prepare the property for the scan?

Stage it exactly how guests will experience it. Make the beds with your best linens, set out the welcome touches, turn on every light, and make sure outdoor spaces are tidy. The 3D tour captures exactly what the camera sees, so treat it like your best guest is about to walk in.

Do you capture outdoor spaces like decks, patios, and hot tubs?

Absolutely. We scan covered porches, decks, patios, pool areas, and outdoor living spaces as part of the tour. For vacation rentals, outdoor amenities are often the biggest selling point. We make sure guests can experience them virtually before booking.

Will the 3D tour actually increase my bookings?

Listings with 3D tours consistently outperform those without. Hosts report up to 14% more bookings, significantly longer listing view times, and fewer pre-booking questions. The tour builds trust and reduces the hesitation that causes guests to bounce to competitor listings.

I manage multiple rental properties. Do you offer volume pricing?

Yes. Property management companies and hosts with multiple listings can take advantage of portfolio pricing. If you are booking 3+ scans, contact us at 864.351.4255 and we will put together a package that makes it easy to add 3D tours across your entire portfolio.

How long does the tour stay online?

Standard hosting is 12 months, which covers a full booking cycle. Renewal options are available and most vacation rental owners keep their tours active year after year since the property layout does not change. You can also transfer the tour to your own Matterport account for free. It is a one-time investment with ongoing returns.

Can you scan between guest turnovers?

Yes. We regularly work around guest schedules and can scan during turnover windows. Most vacation rentals take just 1-2 hours to scan. Just let us know your check-out and check-in times and we will make it work.

Do you serve mountain, lake, and coastal rental areas?

We serve parts of South Carolina, North Carolina and Georgia. That includes popular rental markets like the Blue Ridge mountains, Lake Keowee, Cashiers and Highlands, Asheville, Charleston, Hilton Head, and everywhere in between. We schedule regular trips throughout the week.
