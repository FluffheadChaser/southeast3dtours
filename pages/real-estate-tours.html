Questions Agents Ask Us

How should I prepare the property for the scan?

Stage it like you would for professional photos: declutter, turn on every light, open all blinds, and make sure beds are made. The 3D tour captures exactly what the camera sees, so a well-staged home looks stunning in the final tour.

Can I add the tour to my MLS listing?

Absolutely. You will receive a direct URL that works in every major MLS system. Most agents paste it into the virtual tour field. It also embeds beautifully on your personal website, Zillow, Realtor.com, and social media.

Do buyers need to download an app?

No. The 3D tour runs instantly in any web browser on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Buyers just click the link and start walking through the property. Zero friction.

How long is the tour hosted online?

Standard hosting is 12 months, which covers most listing periods with room to spare. Renewal options are available if you need the tour to stay live longer, for example for a builder's model home. You can also transfer the tour to your own Matterport account for free.

What about vacant or under-construction properties?

3D tours are actually fantastic for vacant homes because they help buyers understand the scale and flow of empty rooms. For new construction, we can scan at multiple stages to document progress and market the finished product.

Do you offer volume pricing for teams or brokerages?

Yes. If you are booking multiple tours per month, reach out and we will put together a package rate that makes it easy to include 3D tours on every listing. Call us at 864.351.4255 to discuss.

Can you add aerial or ground photography?

We are FAA Part 107 certified and can capture professional aerial footage of the property and surrounding area. This is especially popular for homes with large lots, waterfront locations, or mountain views. We also offer DSLR ground-level photography for high-resolution interior and exterior stills. Contact us for add-on pricing on either.

What areas do you serve?

We serve parts of South Carolina, North Carolina and Georgia. Same-day service is available in the greater Greenville area. For locations like Charleston, Asheville, Cashiers, Lake Keowee, and Columbia, we schedule regular trips throughout the week.
