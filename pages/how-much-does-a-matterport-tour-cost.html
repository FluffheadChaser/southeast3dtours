What Happens After 12 Months?

Your tour is hosted on Matterport's cloud for 12 months at no additional cost. When that year is up, you have two options:

Option 1: Transfer to Your Own Account — Free

Move the tour to your own Matterport account at no charge. Matterport offers a free plan that includes one active model — enough for most individual agents or property owners. If you only need to keep one tour live, this costs you nothing ongoing.

Option 2: Continue Hosting Through Us

We can keep your tour live beyond 12 months for an additional fee. This is a good fit for vacation rentals, commercial properties, and ongoing marketing assets. Contact us for current hosting rates.

For most real estate listings, the tour has done its job well before 12 months. For vacation rentals and commercial properties, the free transfer option means you're never locked in.

How Our Pricing Compares

Matterport 3D tour pricing varies significantly across the industry. Nationally, professional Matterport scanning typically falls between $200 and $500 for a standard residential property, with per-square-foot rates ranging from $0.10 to $0.20 depending on your market and provider. Some national companies offer lower base prices but charge separately for hosting, delivery, or add-ons that we include by default.

In South Carolina, most local providers either don't publish their pricing at all or use tiered flat-rate structures that make it difficult to predict your actual cost. Some start charging monthly hosting fees within 60 days of delivery. Others require bundling with photography packages you may not need.

Here's where Southeast 3D Tours stands out:

  • Transparent per-square-foot pricing — you know the cost before you book, not after
  • No hosting fees for 12 months — some SC competitors start charging monthly hosting within 60 days
  • Free transfer to your account — others charge $50 or more for this
  • No mandatory bundles — you're not paying for services you don't need
  • Local operator, not a national call center — your tour is captured and delivered by the same person you book with

What Affects the Cost of a Matterport Tour?

A few variables influence pricing — here's exactly how

Property Size

The biggest variable. More square footage means more scan positions, more processing time, and a larger final model. Our per-square-foot pricing scales naturally — you only pay for the space you need captured.

Property Type & Complexity

An open-floor-plan home scans faster than a multi-room commercial space with hallways, closets, and tight corners. For specialized projects — construction documentation, legal or forensic scanning, or large hospitality spaces — we provide custom quotes.

Add-Ons

Schematic floor plans ($75) and aerial photography ($150) are the most common additions. Neither is required — every tour is complete on its own. Add only what your project calls for.

Turnaround Time

Standard delivery is 24–48 hours. If you need a rush turnaround — same-day or next-morning — call us directly. We can often accommodate depending on our schedule.

Location

Your location within our service area doesn't change the per-square-foot rate. Travel fees may apply for properties outside our core Upstate SC coverage — we'll always tell you upfront if that's the case.

No Surprises

The calculator on this page gives you the real price before you book. For projects that need a custom quote, we'll confirm every cost in writing before any work begins.

Frequently Asked Questions About Matterport Pricing

What is the minimum cost for a Matterport tour?

Our minimum is $300. That covers properties up to approximately 2,100 square feet. For anything larger, the price scales at $0.14 per square foot.

Do you charge for hosting?

Not for the first 12 months. Hosting on Matterport's cloud platform is included with every tour. After that, you can transfer the tour to your own Matterport account for free, or contact us about continued hosting options.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The per-square-foot price is all-inclusive for the standard tour. Add-ons like floor plans and drone photography are optional and priced upfront.

Do I need to buy a Matterport subscription?

Not unless you want to host the tour yourself after the first year. If you do, Matterport offers a free plan that supports one active model — enough for most individual property owners or agents with one listing at a time.

How do I know the exact price for my property?

Use the calculator at the top of this page. Enter your square footage and you'll see your price instantly. If your project has special requirements — construction documentation, legal scanning, large commercial spaces — contact us for a custom quote.

Can I bundle a Matterport tour with drone photography?

Yes. Add aerial photography for $150. We're FAA Part 107 certified for commercial drone operations — it's all handled by the same person, on the same visit, with no third-party coordination needed.

How does your pricing compare to other Matterport providers in South Carolina?

Our per-square-foot pricing is competitive with the industry average and more transparent than most SC providers. We include 12 months of hosting and free account transfers, which many competitors charge extra for. See the comparison section above for details.

What if my property is outside the Greenville area?

We serve a wide area including Greenville, Spartanburg, Asheville, Charleston, Cashiers & Highlands, Lake Keowee, and Columbia. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond our core service area — we'll confirm that when you book.
