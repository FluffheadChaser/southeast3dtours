6 Reasons Commercial Brokers Are Adding 3D Tours to Every Listing

1. Show Multiple Prospects Simultaneously Without Scheduling a Single Tour

Your listing is accessible 24/7 to prospect teams in Charlotte, Atlanta, Nashville, and beyond. A site selection committee of five people can walk through together from their conference room. No coordinating schedules, no property manager meetups, no wasted afternoons.

2. Cut Vacancy Costs by Weeks or Months

Every vacant month on a commercial property costs thousands in lost revenue. 3D tours accelerate the decision cycle by letting tenants evaluate, measure, and share spaces instantly. Faster evaluation means faster lease execution and less money lost to empty square footage.

3. Accurate Measurements That Close Deals

Tenants can measure doorway clearances, ceiling heights, and room dimensions directly inside the 3D tour. Restaurant groups check kitchen layouts. Retailers verify storefront widths. Medical practices confirm exam room sizes. Every number they need is right there, on demand.

4. Win Exclusive Listings From Property Owners

When you walk into a listing pitch with a 3D tour demo of a similar property, owners see the difference immediately. You are not just another broker promising to fill the space. You are the broker with the marketing technology that actually does it.

5. Reduce Tire-Kicker Site Visits by Half

Every in-person tour requires coordination with property managers, security, and sometimes active tenants. When prospects pre-screen in 3D first, they only request in-person visits for spaces they are seriously considering. Your time and your property manager's time become far more productive.

6. Market Spaces That Are Still Occupied

Scan the space while the current tenant is still in place, then market it to new prospects without constant disruption. Or scan it empty to show the raw potential. Either way, the tour is ready to share the moment you need it, without another site visit.

Questions Commercial Clients Ask Us

Can you scan while the space is still occupied by a tenant?

Yes. We regularly scan commercial spaces with active tenants. We coordinate timing to minimize disruption and can work during off-hours, early mornings, or weekends. The scan captures whatever is in the space, so it works whether the property is occupied, staged, or completely vacant.

Does the tour work on LoopNet, CoStar, and CREXi?

Absolutely. You receive a direct URL that can be embedded into any commercial listing platform, your brokerage website, or shared via email. Most platforms have a virtual tour link field designed for exactly this purpose.

How accurate are the built-in measurements?

Matterport measurements are accurate to within 1% under standard conditions. Tenants can measure ceiling heights, doorway widths, room dimensions, and distances between fixtures directly inside the tour. It is precise enough for preliminary space planning.

Can you scan multi-floor buildings?

Yes. We regularly scan multi-story commercial buildings, including individual suites across different floors. Each floor is captured and connected within the same 3D tour, so prospects can navigate the entire property seamlessly.

How large of a space can you scan?

There is no practical upper limit. We have scanned everything from 500 square foot retail spaces to 50,000+ square foot warehouses. Larger spaces simply take more time on site. Contact us for a custom quote on spaces over 10,000 square feet.

Do prospects need to download any software?

No. The 3D tour runs instantly in any web browser on phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Prospects, tenant reps, and entire committees can access it with a single click. Zero friction, zero IT requirements.

Can I get multiple scans of the same space over time?

Yes. Some clients scan a space empty, then again after tenant improvements, and again for marketing a sublease. Each scan is a separate project at standard pricing. We also offer volume discounts for portfolios.

What areas do you serve for commercial scans?

We serve parts of South Carolina, North Carolina and Georgia. Same-day service is available in the greater Greenville area. For locations like Charleston, Columbia, Asheville, and Charlotte, we schedule regular trips throughout the week.
