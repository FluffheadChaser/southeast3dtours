Professional virtual tour services for waterfront homes, lakefront estates, and vacation rentals on South Carolina's premier lake.

Showcasing Lake Keowee's Waterfront Properties

Lake Keowee is one of the most sought-after waterfront destinations in the Southeast, known for crystal-clear water, mountain views, and upscale communities. Waterfront properties here command premium prices and attract buyers from across the country looking for their dream lake home.

We specialize in capturing lakefront properties with Matterport 3D tours that showcase water views, outdoor living spaces, boat docks, and the lifestyle that Lake Keowee offers. Whether you're selling a luxury estate, marketing a vacation rental, or listing a waterfront lot with a view, we help you present your property at its best.

Why 3D Tours Matter for Lake Properties

Showcase Waterfront Views

Photos can't fully capture the lake views and outdoor living spaces that make waterfront properties special. 3D tours let buyers experience the property and the views from every angle.

Out-of-State Buyers

Many Lake Keowee buyers are purchasing second homes or retirement properties from out of state. Virtual tours let them explore properties remotely and narrow their search before visiting in person.

Vacation Rental Marketing

Lake Keowee vacation rentals are highly desirable. Give potential guests a complete tour of your property, including lake access, boat docks, and amenities, to increase bookings.

Premium Property Marketing

Waterfront homes are premium properties that deserve premium marketing. Stand out in the competitive Lake Keowee market with immersive 3D tours that match the quality of your listing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a travel fee for Lake Keowee properties?

No. Lake Keowee is within our standard service area. There's no travel fee for properties around the lake — just standard per-square-foot pricing with a $300 minimum.

Can you include dock structures, covered docks, and outdoor living areas?

Yes. Waterfront features are often the primary selling point of a Lake Keowee property, and we make sure they're captured thoroughly — including docks, boathouses, screened porches, and covered outdoor spaces.

Can a Matterport tour help rent or sell a Lake Keowee vacation property?

Absolutely. A 3D walkthrough gives potential buyers or renters a complete picture of the home, the layout, and the waterfront access before they commit. It reduces questions, builds confidence, and can justify premium listing or rental prices.

Do you work with builders developing on Lake Keowee?

Yes. Builders use Matterport to showcase model homes, spec homes, and completed units to out-of-area buyers who can't easily visit in person. It's a strong tool for pre-sales and new construction marketing on the lake.
