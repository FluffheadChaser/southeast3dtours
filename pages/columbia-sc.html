Serving Columbia & the Midlands

As South Carolina's capital and largest city, Columbia's real estate market is diverse and dynamic. From historic homes in Shandon and Rosewood to new construction in Blythewood, lakefront properties on Lake Murray, and commercial spaces downtown—properties throughout the Midlands benefit from modern virtual tour technology.

We provide professional Matterport 3D tours for residential listings, commercial properties, vacation rentals, and investment properties throughout the Columbia metro area and surrounding counties.

Why 3D Tours Matter in Columbia

Growing Market

Columbia's real estate market continues to grow with population increases and economic development. Stand out from competing listings with immersive 3D tours that engage buyers.

Military & USC Relocations

Fort Jackson and the University of South Carolina bring constant relocation activity. Virtual tours help remote buyers explore properties before they arrive in Columbia.

Investment Properties

Columbia has an active rental and investment property market. 3D tours help showcase rental properties to potential tenants and investment properties to out-of-town buyers.

Commercial Real Estate

As the state capital, Columbia has diverse commercial real estate needs. Virtual tours help lease office space, retail locations, and restaurant spaces more efficiently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a travel fee for Columbia?

Yes. Columbia is outside our standard service area, so a travel fee applies. Contact us for exact pricing based on your property's location in the Midlands — we're transparent about the cost upfront.

Is the travel fee worth it for Columbia listings?

For most Columbia properties, yes — particularly investment properties, higher-end residential listings, and commercial spaces. Contact us to discuss whether it makes sense for your specific situation.

Can Matterport help manage investment and rental properties remotely?

Yes. Property managers and investors use Matterport to pre-qualify prospective tenants remotely, document property condition between leases, and market vacant units — reducing the need for in-person showings and saving significant time.

Do you work with military relocation and university-related moves?

Yes. Agents working with Fort Jackson PCS moves or USC faculty and staff relocations find that 3D tours significantly accelerate the decision process for buyers moving from out of state who can't easily preview properties in advance.
