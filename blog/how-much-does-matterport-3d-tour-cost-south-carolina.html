How Much Does a Matterport 3D Tour Cost in South Carolina?

It's the first question most people ask, and we think it deserves a straight answer.

A Matterport 3D tour from Southeast 3D Tours costs $0.14 per square foot, with a $300 minimum. That's it. No tiered packages to compare, no "contact us for a quote," no surprise fees after the fact.

A 2,000 square foot home? $300. A 3,500 square foot listing? $490. A 10,000 square foot commercial space? $1,400. You can run your exact number on our pricing calculator before you ever pick up the phone.

Why We Price This Way

Most Matterport providers use tiered packages — small, medium, large — and every property ends up in an awkward middle ground where you're either overpaying for a package you don't need or upgrading to one that barely fits. We skipped all of that.

Square footage is the only variable that matters in a 3D scan. Bigger spaces take more scan positions and more processing time. Smaller spaces take less. Pricing by the square foot means you pay exactly for what you get, and you know the number before we show up.

The Real Question Isn't What It Costs — It's What It Returns

A $350 tour on a $400,000 listing is less than 0.1% of the sale price. Here's what that fraction of a percent actually buys you.

That investment saves you 5 to 10 hours in wasted showings from unqualified buyers who would have filtered themselves out after walking through the tour online. It generates 95% more inquiries on your listing. Properties with 3D tours sell 31% faster on average. And when you sit down at your next listing presentation with a Matterport tour in your marketing plan, the impression it makes on a seller is the kind of thing you can't put a price on.

This math works the same way whether you're a real estate agent trying to stand out in a crowded spring market, a vacation rental host looking to increase bookings, or a commercial property owner marketing a space to out-of-state tenants. The cost is small. The upside is not.

What's Included

Every scan includes the full interactive 3D tour with walkthrough navigation, dollhouse view, schematic floor plans, and the built-in measurement tool. You get a shareable link that works on any device — no app required. Embed it on your website, drop it in an MLS listing, text it to a client, or use it in an email campaign. It's yours to use however you want, for as long as you want.

We Serve the Entire Upstate and Beyond

We're based in Travelers Rest, SC and serve clients throughout Greenville, Asheville, Charleston, Cashiers & Highlands, Lake Keowee, and Columbia. Same pricing everywhere. No travel fees within our service areas.
